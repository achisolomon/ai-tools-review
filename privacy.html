You have several options to control personalized advertising:

  • Google Ad Settings: Manage your ad preferences
  • NAI Opt-out: Network Advertising Initiative opt-out

If you opt out, you'll still see ads, but they won't be personalized to your interests.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

You have the right to withdraw consent at any time. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
